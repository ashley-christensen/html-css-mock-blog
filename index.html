What is a Self-Taught Developer?

A self-taught Developer is a self-starter who also enjoys learning. They are a person who wants to make the most out of online content, and learn web programming, applications, and more.

Developers consist of elements that describe a type of person including persistent, innovative, disciplined, motivated, and bright. Employers who see these developers tend to hire them.

Developers are made up of 3 parts:

  1. The starters
  2. The consistent
  3. Those who actually like it
